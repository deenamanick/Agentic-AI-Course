# Practical 12.4 — Human Approval and Runtime Budgets

## Build

Preview a simulated email or payment action, pause execution, and require approval from an authenticated human. Add maximum steps, tokens, duration, retries, and spend.

## Success checklist

- [ ] The model cannot self-approve.
- [ ] Edited actions require renewed approval.
- [ ] Expired approvals cannot execute.
- [ ] Budget exhaustion ends safely.
